Swedish Pancakes Recipe

Swedish pancakes, also known as pannkaka, are a flat, fried cake that is much thinner than a typical pancake but thicker than a crepe.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups milk
  • 1 1/2 cups flower
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon baking powder
  • 3 eggs
  • 1/2 cup butter

Instructions

  1. Mix all ingredients together
  2. Heat a non-stick skillet over medium heat. Grease it with butter.
  3. Pour a small amount of batter in the center of the skillet. Make sure it is a thin layer.
  4. Cook until solidified and flip it over. It should be lightly browned on each side. Set them aside once cooked.
